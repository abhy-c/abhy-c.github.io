SignalBoard is a concept project centered on one question: how should a weekly product review feel if the goal is better judgment rather than more charts?

## Product framing

Many dashboards optimize for access to data. Far fewer optimize for interpretation. The concept here was to make each weekly review easier to answer in plain language:

- What changed?
- Why did it change?
- Who owns the next response?

## Design choice

Instead of adding more widgets, the concept narrows the view to a small set of metrics and wraps each one in definition, recent movement, and recommended next attention area.

That tradeoff reduces ambiguity and makes the review process more useful for cross-functional stakeholders who do not live inside dashboards all day.
