PolicyPath explores the gap between internal compliance requirements and the external user experience they often create.

## Core idea

The project starts from a simple premise: users are more tolerant of friction when the friction feels purposeful, predictable, and explainable.

## Product implications

That leads to a few design priorities:

- explain why information is being requested
- distinguish required next steps from review states
- give support and escalation clearer space in the journey

The result is not a looser workflow. It is a workflow that behaves more honestly.
