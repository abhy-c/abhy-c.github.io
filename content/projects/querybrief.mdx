QueryBrief treats analytics output as unfinished work until it becomes legible to the people making the decision.

## Why it matters

SQL answers are not automatically strategic answers. Teams still need a small narrative layer:

- what was asked
- what was found
- what remains uncertain
- what decision should happen next

That narrative discipline often matters more than adding another chart.
