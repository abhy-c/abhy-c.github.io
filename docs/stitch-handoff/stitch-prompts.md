# Stitch Prompts

These prompts are written for the current portfolio site and are designed to preserve the existing positioning while giving Stitch enough direction to improve composition and polish.

## Master Prompt

```text
Design a premium personal portfolio website for Abhyudai Chauhan, a product manager with an NYU MBA (STEM), an ex-attorney background, and expertise across AI, SQL, fintech, regulated systems, and product execution.

The site should feel credible, calm, polished, editorial, and business-ready. Avoid generic startup SaaS aesthetics, purple AI visuals, playful illustration systems, or flashy trends. The experience should feel like a high-signal strategy portfolio: restrained, intelligent, and trust-building.

Preserve the core information architecture:
- Home
- About
- Projects
- Writing
- Consulting

Preserve the core business goals:
- establish credibility within 5 to 10 seconds
- communicate a clear cross-functional profile
- showcase selected projects and long-form writing
- create a low-friction path to book one free consult

Use this palette as the base:
- background #f6f3ee
- foreground #000000
- surface #ffffff
- muted text #1f2937
- border #c7d4d8
- teal accent #0f8b8d
- bronze accent #b08a3e

Use a polished sans-serif primary typography voice with selective editorial serif contrast. Keep rounded cards, soft borders, subtle shadows, and generous spacing. Improve hierarchy and composition without changing the professional positioning.
```

## Homepage Prompt

```text
Create a desktop-first homepage for Abhyudai Chauhan's portfolio website, with a mobile-responsive layout. The homepage should immediately communicate this positioning:

"Building clear product strategy for AI, fintech, and regulated systems."

The page should include:
- a premium hero section with an eyebrow, large headline, summary, supporting paragraph, and two CTAs
- visible credibility markers such as NYU MBA (STEM), ex-attorney, B2B and fintech, AI / SQL / blockchain
- a featured writing section with curated article cards
- a featured project gallery with curated case-study style cards
- a consulting section promoting one free consult as the launch offer

The tone should be strategic, composed, and highly credible. Keep the design editorial and bespoke rather than looking like a template. Use the warm neutral background, white rounded cards, teal accent, and selective bronze support accent. Add enough asymmetry and composition confidence to feel premium, but keep the content easy to scan.
```

## About Page Prompt

```text
Design an About page for a premium portfolio site. The page is for Abhyudai Chauhan, a product manager with an NYU MBA (STEM), an ex-attorney background, and a focus on AI, product strategy, fintech, and data-informed execution.

The page should include:
- a strong introduction section with professional headshot
- a concise explanation of the cross-functional background
- a short timeline or structured set of career strengths
- a section on working style and operating principles
- a final CTA area pointing to consulting and resume access

The tone should be thoughtful, assured, and operator-led. Avoid making the page feel like a resume dump. It should feel curated, high-signal, and polished.
```

## Projects Page Prompt

```text
Design a Projects page for a curated portfolio. This is not a giant grid of side projects. It should present a selective set of independent concept builds that demonstrate product framing, operating thinking, analytics fluency, and fintech or regulated-systems judgment.

Current projects include:
- PolicyPath
- QueryBrief
- SignalBoard

The page should feel selective and high-signal. Use cards or modular layouts that emphasize project title, summary, category, and strategic framing. Make it easy for a hiring manager or consulting lead to understand the thinking behind each project quickly.
```

## Writing Page Prompt

```text
Design a Writing page for a professional portfolio site. The page should showcase long-form essays on product strategy, AI, analytics, legal reasoning, and fintech decision making.

Current article topics include:
- Designing AI Features That Earn Trust
- What Legal Reasoning Adds to Product Strategy
- When SQL Becomes a Product Skill

The page should feel editorial, readable, and substantial. It should signal discipline and thoughtfulness rather than content marketing. Prioritize readability, good metadata hierarchy, and a curated archive feel.
```

## Consulting Page Prompt

```text
Design a Consulting page for a portfolio site with one clear launch offer: a free consult. The page should feel practical and professional, not salesy.

The page should include:
- a strong intro explaining the free consult
- a section describing who this is for
- a clear list of example use cases across product strategy, AI opportunities, SQL-backed decision making, and fintech or regulated product questions
- a section showing future consulting offer categories without making them active yet
- a booking area that can either show a live embed or a clean placeholder state

The design should make the CTA obvious while preserving trust and restraint.
```

## Variant Prompt

```text
Generate 3 variants of this page. Keep the same content structure, professional tone, and warm premium palette, but explore different layout composition and hierarchy approaches. Do not introduce purple AI styling, dark cyber aesthetics, or playful consumer-product visuals.
```

## Refinement Prompt

```text
Refine this design by improving hierarchy, spacing, typography contrast, and CTA clarity. Keep the design restrained and executive. Make it feel more bespoke and more premium without making it louder.
```
